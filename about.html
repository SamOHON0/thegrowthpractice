About The Growth Practice

Eavann Murphy

Executive and leadership coach, EMCC accredited, with 30+ years of senior commercial leadership behind the chair.

Psychological insight, commercial reality.

The Growth Practice is an executive coaching and leadership development practice based in Dublin. It was founded by Eavann Murphy, a seasoned commercial leader with a track record in designing market strategies and executing successful go-to-market outcomes.

I have led large teams and major commercial transformations in regulated, high-profile sectors, managing diverse stakeholder expectations across boards, regulators, customers, and internal functions. This lived experience of delivering results under pressure, navigating politics, and holding P&L accountability is central to how I coach.

I partner with executives, emerging leaders, and leadership teams working through complexity, transformation, and high-visibility roles, so they can grow in a way that is both commercially effective and personally sustainable.
