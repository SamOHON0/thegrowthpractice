The Approach

My approach

Development in real time, focused on your live leadership challenges rather than abstract theory.

Read on

What I help leaders develop

Three capabilities that compound as scope and stakes grow, developed in real time against live leadership challenges rather than abstract theory.

Self-awareness

Understanding your patterns, triggers, strengths, and impact on others, so you can see yourself more clearly and choose how you show up, rather than reacting on autopilot.

Relational skill

Building trust, working with tension, and leading difficult conversations with clarity, so you can align people, influence effectively, and get work done through others.

Resilience under pressure

Staying grounded, thinking clearly, and sustaining performance when the stakes are high, so you can lead when the going gets tough without losing your edge.

How I work

I coach leaders at all levels who want to lead with greater clarity, stronger relationships, and more impact. Our work is practical and rooted in the realities of leadership: complex decisions, organisational dynamics, performance pressure, and the challenge of getting work done through others.

Sessions are anchored in what is happening now, a live issue, a key relationship, a decision under pressure, or a repeating leadership pattern. The aim is not insight for its own sake, but meaningful change in how you lead, relate, and deliver results.

Evidence-based, commercially grounded

My coaching integrates established psychological approaches with the lived experience of holding accountability in complex, high-pressure roles. That means the work is both developmentally robust and commercially relevant.

It is evidence-based without being abstract, psychologically informed without losing practicality, and focused on shifts that the people around you can feel and the organisation can see.
