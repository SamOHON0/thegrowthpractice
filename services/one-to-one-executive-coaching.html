Service 01

One-to-one executive coaching

Confidential one-to-one coaching for senior leaders navigating critical deliverables, complexity, or the stretch roles that test them most.

Who it is for

Senior leaders carrying real accountability: executives stepping up in scope, leaders facing a high-stakes deliverable, and people performing in roles that stretch them beyond what got them here.

What we focus on

The work is anchored in what is happening now, a live issue, a key relationship, a decision under pressure, or a repeating leadership pattern. Over the programme we build:

  • Self-awareness — your patterns, triggers, strengths, and the impact you have on others.
  • Impact — how you show up, influence, and get work done through other people.
  • Resilience — staying grounded and thinking clearly when the stakes are high.

How it works

A typical engagement runs across six sessions, giving us time to move from insight to durable change rather than a one-off conversation. Sessions are confidential, practical, and grounded in the realities of your role. The aim is not insight for its own sake, but shifts the people around you can feel and the organisation can see.
